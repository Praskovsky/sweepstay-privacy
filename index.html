Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the Sweepstay app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Praskovsky (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Freemium service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".


Information Collection and Use

The Application does not collect personally identifiable information. No user accounts, logins, or profiles are created. Scan results (Wi-Fi devices, Bluetooth devices) are processed entirely on your device and are never uploaded to any server.

The Application requires access to your device's location permission. On Android, this permission is required by the operating system to scan nearby Wi-Fi networks and Bluetooth devices. The Application does not collect, store, or transmit your GPS location.

When you request AI analysis of a suspicious device, the Application sends a device fingerprint (consisting of the device's network vendor, open ports, service announcements, and any network name it broadcasts — none of which identify you) to a secure proxy server, which forwards it to an AI service (Groq) for analysis. A nearby device's broadcast network name may occasionally contain its owner's name; this information describes other devices in your environment, not you, is not linked to your identity, and is not stored after the response is returned.

The Application collects crash reports automatically via Sentry. These reports may include your device model, Android OS version, and a technical stack trace. They do not include personal information or scan results.

In-app purchases are handled by RevenueCat. Purchase transaction data (e.g. purchase receipt) is processed by RevenueCat to verify your Pro entitlement. The Service Provider does not store payment details.


Third Party Access

The Service Provider does not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. The Application uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policy:

  • Google Play Services
  • Sentry — crash reporting
  • RevenueCat — in-app purchases

The Service Provider may disclose information:

  • as required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, or similar legal process;
  • when they believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect their rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request.

Opt-Out Rights

You can stop all data processing by uninstalling the Application. You may use the standard uninstall processes available on your mobile device or via the Google Play Store.


Data Retention Policy

The Application does not retain scan data — all results are stored locally on your device and are cleared when you uninstall the Application. Crash reports retained by Sentry and purchase records retained by RevenueCat are subject to their respective data retention policies. To request deletion of any data, please contact the Service Provider at sweepstayapp@gmail.com.


Children

The Service Provider does not use the Application to knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13. The Application does not address anyone under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided personal information, please contact the Service Provider at sweepstayapp@gmail.com.


Security

AI enrichment requests are transmitted over HTTPS. The Service Provider does not store personal data on its servers. Crash reports and purchase records are handled by Sentry and RevenueCat respectively, both of which maintain their own security practices.
